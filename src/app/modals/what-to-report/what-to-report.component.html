What to Report

For law enforcement to open an investigation on your tip, they need “actionable information.” This would include:

Cars

Are there any vehicles involved in the suspects activity? Record the make, model, color, license plate number, etc.

People

Who is involved in the activity? Make note of height, weight, hair color, eye color, age, etc.

Date & Time

When did the suspicious activity happen? What day and what time?

Location

Where did the suspicious activity take place? Do you have an address? Be as specific as you can.
