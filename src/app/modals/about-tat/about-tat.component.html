About Truckers Against Trafficking

We exist to educate, equip, empower, and mobilize members of the trucking and busing industries to combat domestic sex trafficking as a regular part of their jobs.

Our three goals are

  • Saturate trucking and related industries with TAT materials
  • Partner with law enforcement and government agencies to facilitate the investigation of human trafficking
  • Marshal the resources of our partners to combat this crime

Learn about the impact TAT and our partners made in 2017 through our Annual Report.
