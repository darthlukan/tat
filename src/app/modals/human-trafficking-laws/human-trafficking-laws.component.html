Human Trafficking Laws

Did you know?

Human trafficking is the recruitment, transportation, transfer, harboring or obtaining of persons through force, fraud and coercion for the purpose of exploitation.

  • All 50 states and D.C. have a law criminalizing sex trafficking
  • 31 states have a buyer applicable trafficking law that prohibits a mistake of age defense in prosecutions for buying a commercial sex act with any minor under 18
  • Some states punish sex purchasers the same as traffickers, generally with felony level crimes
  • Washington identifies anyone who purchases a sexually explicit act as a human trafficker guilty of a class-A felony
  • There is a lifetime ban on a CDL for any individual who uses their CMV in committing a felony involving a severe form of human trafficking
